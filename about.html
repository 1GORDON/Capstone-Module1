CCS Summer Concert 2021

The CCS Summer Concert was started in Uganda a few years ago, that is, 2018

These kinds of concerts were started to address the issues kids face and which could hinder their mental state as they grow. In order to improve the health of Ugandan children, experts and specialists from Mulago hospitala with support from other international institutions like Save The Child encouraged the inception of this concert. This effort came to life in order to protect childrens health by increasing body activity
